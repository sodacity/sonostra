Objective

Complete the arrow sequences that appear on your grid before the timer runs out. Completing sequences in Wave Mode damages the boss. Failing a sequence will cost you a life!

Controls

Use the arrow keys on your keyboard that correspond to the arrows on screen. On mobile devices, you can swipe in the direction of the arrow.
